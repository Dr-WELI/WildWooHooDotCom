Approach

Learning through story, play, and connection to nature

Our educational work builds on the same creative logic as our music and visual projects: ideas are translated into formats that children can feel, understand, and engage with.

Nature, imagination, and storytelling are used as entry points to help children explore belonging, diversity, and their relationship to the world around them.
