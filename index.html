About

WildWooHoo builds creative worlds where nature, emotion and culture meet for social change

We make audio-visual products and experiences that help people feel closer to the natural world and more aware we are an extension of it

Founded by Dr WELI, WildWooHoo explores how music, visual storytelling, and nature-inspired narratives can create new ways of thinking about belonging, diversity, and social change

Impact focus

Two areas of social impact

01

Natural diversity and our relationship with one another

We explore how individual differences, group life, cooperation, and social behaviour shape the way we relate to each other, often using animal societies as a mirror for human life

02

Changing environments and our belonging to nature

We create work that reconnects people with the understanding we are part of the animal kingdom and part of nature, especially in times of environmental change, uncertainty, and disconnection

Creative process

How we create

01

Nature as muse

We begin with natural behaviour, animal societies, ecosystems, or environmental feeling

02

Emotion as entry point

03

Interconnected storytelling

04

Creative work with message

The outcome can become music, music videos, educational concepts, science communication, or visual experiences carrying social impact
