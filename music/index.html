About the Work

Dr WELI has spent over a decade in Australia as a researcher, educator, and creative. His project Kangaroo Time reinterprets scientific research through music and dance, reaching global audiences and generating widespread media attention.

The work reveals a key insight: when environmental knowledge is translated into culturally resonant forms, people respond emotionally.
